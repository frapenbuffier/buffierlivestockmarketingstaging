About Us

Buffier Livestock Marketing was established July 2015 by licensed Stock & Station Agent, Francis Buffier. With over 20 years of experience in the cattle industry & over 10 years in agency practice, Francis has a strong network of producers & buyers within the industry.

In July 2017, Buffier Livestock Marketing purchased Rockwell Livestock, Merriwa and has expanded its cattle producer connections across the region.

Buffier Livestock Marketing is a member of the Australian Livestock & Property Agents Association (ALPA), the National Industry body for livestock and property agents.
